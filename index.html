BIOGRAPHY

I am a UX/UI designer, media expert, and creative web developer currently working with a strong and motivated team at MEG Support Tools and patientMpower. I specialize in bringing concepts and ideas for mobile applications from sketch to completion, following and tayloring every stage of the process: from initial data models and screen-flows to completed product.

I work with a number of tools, most prominently Sketch, Zeplin, Illustrator and Balsamiq Mockups. Due to my past career in audio production, I also have a lot of experience with audio and video editing software.
