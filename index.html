The Irish Times

The Irish Times was the first Irish newspaper, and one of the first in the world, to "go digital". It is a long-standing news agency that has evolved much in over 100 years of existence, and today focuses on delivering real, reliable information in an era dominated by conspiracy theories and "fake news". Working on such a well respected and renowned news source is challenging, and has tought me a lot.

INTERACTIONS

Here you can view and engage with interactions I created for hybrid mobile applications.

BIOGRAPHY

I am a UX/UI designer that specializes in bringing concepts and ideas for mobile applications and websites from sketch to completion, following and tailoring every stage of the process: from initial data models and screen-flows to completed product.

I work with a number of tools, most prominently Sketch, Zeplin, Illustrator and Balsamiq Mockups, but I learn new software quickly. Due to my past career in audio production, I also have a lot of experience with audio and video editing software.

When I am not working I enjoy practicing Muay Thai, playing video games, reading, and hiking. I also have a passion for style and sartorialism.

I built this website without using any UI or animations libraries such as Bootstrap, jQuery UI or ScrollMagic. All the animations, interactions and styling were designed and created by me using CSS3 and some jQuery to trigger the animations.
