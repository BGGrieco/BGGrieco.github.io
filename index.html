BIOGRAPHY

I am a UX/UI designer currently working with a strong and motivated team at MEG Support Tools and patientMpower. I specialize in bringing concepts and ideas for mobile applications from sketch to completion, following and tailoring every stage of the process: from initial data models and screen-flows to completed product.

I work with a number of tools, most prominently Sketch, Zeplin, Illustrator and Balsamiq Mockups. Due to my past career in audio production, I also have a lot of experience with audio and video editing software.

MEG Audit Tool

A showcase of the work I have done on MEG Support Tools' Audit Tool.

The MEG Audit Tool is a data collection tool built with Angular 6. As such, it runs on any mobile device and browser, and empowers data collection for hospitals, building sites and any situation that requires auditing or data collection.

The video below shows the evolution of flow and UI design in the product.

Responsibilities on this project:
- Flow design
- UX/UI design
- Mock-ups and testing
- Front-end development
- Team management

patientMpower

A showcase of the work I have done for patientMpower.

patientMpower is a data collection app that focuses on empowering patients to directly record quantitative and qualitative data on their condition and share it with their carers and doctors directly. This helps patients better manage their chronic disease, improving their outcomes & providing data to help further medical research.

The video below shows the evolution of flow and UI design in the product.

Responsibilities on this project:
- Flow design
- UX/UI design
- Mock-ups and testing

Casa dello Zecchiere

A showcase of the website for Casa dello Zecchiere, which I designed and developed.

Casa dello Zecchiere is a venue situated in a beautiful hystorical building in Milan city centre. Available to book for parties, conferences, art shows, fundraisers etc.

Responsibilities on this project:
- Flow design
- UX/UI design
- Full stack development
